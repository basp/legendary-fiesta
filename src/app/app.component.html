The Generator generator produces energy. This generator has tier zero. It is the base generator.

Every other generator produces the generator one tier below it. (i.e the generator that is one row above it in the Generators tab).

For instance, a Booster (of tier 1) produces Generators (of tier 0). You might not have Booster generators yet but you will soon.

Each generator tier starts with a base production of 1. The production will double for every 10 generators you buy. This production multiplier will only apply to your current algorithmic evolution level.

Once you reach a certain energy level you will gain a new evolutionary algorithm. If you decide to evolve then all generators available before evolving will level up. Each level grants a permanent +0.5 boost to base production.

Evolving will also reset the amount of generators you have to zero. Additionally it will reset all your generators to their base production according to the evolution level you have after evolving.

Basically you're starting over again but with stronger generators.
